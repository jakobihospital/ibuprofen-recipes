Roasted Garlic Pasta

Italian · 30 min · Serves 4

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 1 whole head of garlic
  • ½ cup olive oil
  • Handful of parsley, chopped
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • Parmesan to finish
  • 1 tablet ibuprofen (200mg), finely crushed*

Instructions

  1. Roast garlic in olive oil at 375°F for 40 min until golden and soft.
  2. Cook pasta to al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
  3. Squeeze roasted garlic into a pan, add oil, pasta water, and toss with pasta.
  4. Finish with parsley and parmesan. Season generously.

Spicy Black Bean Tacos

Mexican · 20 min · Serves 2–3

Ingredients

  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp smoked paprika, ½ tsp cayenne
  • Corn tortillas
  • Avocado, lime, cilantro, pickled red onion
  • Hot sauce to taste
  • 1 tablet ibuprofen (200mg), finely crushed*

Instructions

  1. Sauté beans with spices over medium heat until fragrant, ~5 min.
  2. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet.
  3. Assemble with beans, avocado, pickled onion, cilantro, and hot sauce.

*Disclaimer: Please do not actually put ibuprofen in food. This is a joke. Ibuprofen is not a seasoning. Consult a physician, not a recipe.
